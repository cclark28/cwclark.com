About

I'm a multidisciplinary designer based in Indianapolis, Indiana, creating digital experiences and print collateral that resonate with audiences. When I'm not behind a screen, I’m screen printing, crafting experimental motion graphics, capturing moments through photography, or illustrating immersive worlds within my comic books.
