Delivra

Delivra uses email marketing tools and strategies to help you have more conversations and make more sales. Delivra needed a clean reboot of their email, website, and brand. Given the base topic of conversations, Blend Creative Team developed a series of concepts and delivered a new website along with the tools to help Delivra maintain its modern look.

Refreshed logo

A new logo was developed with a speech bubble on the “i” to indicate communication. In email marketing, communication between businesses and consumers is key.

Brand palette update

﻿﻿The main color scheme represents a bright and colorful appearance. The primary goal was to give Delivra a clean, modern pop feel that would brighten anyone’s mood throughout the day.
