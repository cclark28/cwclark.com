At Terminus, I’ve been part of a journey that goes beyond just offering a platform—we’re pioneering the future of account-based marketing (ABM). As the original creators of ABM, I’ve had the privilege of working with a team that’s deeply committed to driving real, sustainable revenue growth for businesses. Our SaaS platform isn’t just about meeting goals; it’s about surpassing them and making a lasting impact.

Throughout my time here, I’ve seen firsthand how our cutting-edge advertising, first-party data, and multi-channel engagement have transformed over 500 companies into powerful growth engines. Clients like Gainsight, Roche, and Dow Jones have trusted us to elevate their marketing strategies, helping us maintain our position as the leading ABM platform on G2 for 17 straight quarters.

At Terminus, I’ve worked as both Brand Designer and Senior Creative Designer, contributing to shaping our creative direction alongside a talented team of marketers. I’ve been involved in key projects like advertisements, ebooks, and digital campaigns. I also played a significant role in a major brand overhaul, refreshing our standards to enhance Terminus’s identity and support its growth. From OOH advertising to trade show displays, my focus has been on creating impactful visual experiences.

Site Refresh

To start the new year with a fresh outlook, we gave Terminus.com a complete makeover. Our goal was to revitalize the brand by creating a sleek, modern website. Collaborating closely with our Content team and Director of Marketing, we focused on enhancing the site’s functionality, refining our messaging, and most importantly, elevating the user experience. We broke the refresh process into stages to ensure a smooth handoff to our web development agency, allowing for thorough testing and adjustments to the user flow before implementation.

Style Sheets were implemented to enhance the performance of our buttons and streamline typography for a polished and contemporary brand appearance. This will enable us to enforce consistency throughout the entire website.
